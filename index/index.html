Welcome

Welcome to Gaming Galaxy. This website is created for people that are interested in getting started in gaming or eSports. The website contains on-site written guides aswell as external resource links. You can navigate through our site using the Navagation menu found on the left of this homepage, or click on the pictures below in the Featured section. Besides guides, we also provide links to Live Streaming websites such as Twitch and Community websites such as Reddit on the top section of this webpage. Learning how to play a new video game is time consuming and often difficult. We advise you to always maintain a positive attitude when gaming and most importantly, have fun!
