The lastest installment CounterStrike:Global Offensive has recieved many FPS fans' appreciation. The CounterStrike series has recentenly gained popularity on the market as well as making its apperance on the eSports scene. In order to become a good CS player, one must be able to have good mechanical skills, decision making skills and communication skills.

Basic Gameplay

You have already configured the game and set your mouse sens, its time to hop into a match.
You can pick an official matching making button by clicking on the top left of the dashboard.

We highly recommend you to start by playing the Deathmatch mode, which is a mode that is primirarly focused on killing the enemy player with no other objectives present.

Once you are comfortable shooting at the enemy players its time to try a casual demolition mode, where the terrorists team attempt to plant and detonate the bomb(C4) on either A or B site, and the counter-terrorists team attempt to stop them. The recommended beginner maps are Dust_2 and Mirage since there is a contrast between them. Dust_2 has open spaces and allows for gun play compared to Mirage which is more suited for stealth and flanking. These 2 maps are also the most played maps in the history of CounterStrike.
