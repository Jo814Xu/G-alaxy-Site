The ancient town of the mountainous region, Yharnam, is a cursed place. The unique local diseases of the plague of monsters have been circulating since ancient times. As in the name of a disease, a patient suffering from this condition will become a beast that loses his mind as a disease name. Most patients with this disease travel long distances to Yharnam and seek ways to rescue them.
