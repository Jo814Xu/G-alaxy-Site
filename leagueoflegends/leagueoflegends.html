League of Legends

-- LOL

League of Legends made by Riot Games is a MOBA released in 2009 that took heavy inspirations from Defense of the Ancients. League of legends is very well known for its competative scenes and is also ranked first for viewership in many different types of media and streaming services. In all game modes, players control characters called champions, who each have a set of unique abilities to help themselves or their team to defeat their enemies. Aside from the gameplay aspect, the artworks and designs of these champions also varies and blends which keeps the game interesting.

When learning League of Legends it is again important to practice some of the basic skills mentioned in the basic MOBA skills guide such as lasthitting, map awarness etc. Practice makes perfect, but when you are struggling in the game and you are on a losing streak, you should take some breaks and look at the skill descriptions of each champions. There are over 137 champions in league of Legends and once you learn about how all of them interact with the game you will notice some major improvements of your own skills even if you don't play or don't have certain champions.
